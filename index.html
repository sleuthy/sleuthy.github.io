About Me

Hi there! I'm a web developer with experience in front and backend environments, especially interested in creating accessible, usable software that enhances people's lives. I've worked on mobile and web applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Liquid, amongst other languages and frameworks. I love expanding my knowledge while honing my existing skillset.

Since 2005, I have worked in the investigative industry. In 2008, I built an investigative research business in which I cater to attorneys and corporate professionals, provide due diligence, conduct background investigations, and perform complex research inquiries and data analysis.

I also have experience in higher education, where I worked as an accessibility advocate for college students, ensuring compliancy with ADA and Section 504/508 guidelines while offering academic coaching and advising to students with disabilities.

I'm seeking opportunities in team-driven, collaborative environments. Check out my resume, my code, and send me an email! I'd love to hear from you.
